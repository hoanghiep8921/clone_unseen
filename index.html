ABOUT ME

Art is the language I breathe in. I create to decipher the pulse of human emotions and the way individuality bends or endures under pressure.
I am endlessly curious about the stories stitched into history and culture, tracing the causes behind why we become and why we feel.
Between past and present, heart and mind, I make art to understand, and to celebrate the shapes of being human
