💡 TỐI ƯU THÊM
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Enable GZIP Compression (.htaccess):
   <IfModule mod_deflate.c>
     AddOutputFilterByType DEFLATE text/javascript
   </IfModule>
   → Giảm thêm ~70% khi transfer

2. Enable Browser Caching (.htaccess):
   <FilesMatch "\.(js)$">
     Header set Cache-Control "max-age=31536000, public"
   </FilesMatch>

3. Preload Critical Resources (HTML <head>):
   <link rel="preload" href="/path/to/vendor.js" as="script">
